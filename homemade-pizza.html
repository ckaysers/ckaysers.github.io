Recipe of the Day: Homemade Pizza

By Ellen Kinsey

For those seeking a truly scrumptious, homemade pizza experience, look no further! In this step-by-step guide, we'll unveil the secrets to crafting the perfect pizza, right from the comfort of your own kitchen. With just a few simple ingredients and a touch of culinary prowess, you'll be whipping up mouth-watering pizzas in no time.

Ingredients

  • 250g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 150ml warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 200g tomato passata
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1-2 tbsp grated Parmesan
  • 125g mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by preparing the dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine the strong bread flour, instant yeast, and salt. Gradually add the warm water, stirring until the mixture forms a soft, pliable dough. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Drizzle with 1 tbsp of olive oil, cover with a damp cloth, and leave in a warm place to rise for 1-2 hours.
  2. Preheat your oven to 240°C (220°C for fan ovens) or Gas Mark 9. Meanwhile, prepare the tomato sauce by gently simmering the tomato passata, crushed garlic, and dried basil for 10-15 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper, then set aside to cool.
  3. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down to remove any air bubbles, and then transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Roll out the dough to your desired thickness and shape, and place it onto a parchment-lined baking tray.
  4. Spread the cooled tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan, and top with sliced mozzarella.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned. Remove from the oven, and allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before garnishing with fresh basil leaves.
  6. Slice the pizza into even portions and serve immediately. Enjoy your homemade pizza, brimming with authentic flavours and a delightfully crisp crust.

Feeling adventurous? Don't hesitate to get creative with your pizza toppings! From classic Margherita to enticing gourmet combinations, the possibilities are truly endless. Remember, the key to a perfect pizza lies in the quality of the ingredients and the love you put into making it.

Happy pizza making, and bon appétit!
